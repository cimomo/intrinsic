Intrinsic

From ticker to thesis.

A Claude Code plugin for equity research and DCF valuation — with built-in checks that challenge your assumptions before you commit to them.

claude plugin install intrinsic

Why not just ask Claude?

You could. But plain-session analysis has blind spots.

Real data

Plain Claude invents numbers. Intrinsic fetches real financials from Alpha Vantage and caches them for reuse.

Structured pipeline

Claude skips steps when it's in a hurry. The five-step pipeline can't cut corners — each step depends on the prior.

Self-challenge

Claude won't push back on its own conclusions. Intrinsic has coherence checks, sensitivity analysis, and pre-mortem reasoning.

Persistence

Assumptions vanish when the session ends. Intrinsic saves them per stock, with manual overrides that survive across runs.

Built-in rigor

Three mechanisms that keep your analysis honest.

Reverse DCF

Before you set a single assumption, the tool calculates what growth rate the current price already implies. If you're more bullish than the market, you'll know exactly where you diverge.

Coherence & Sensitivity

After all assumptions are set, the tool checks them against each other and against your research signals. Then it shows which assumption has the biggest impact on fair value.

Confidence-Adjusted

Recommendations downgrade automatically when research confidence is low. A 20% upside becomes HOLD instead of BUY when the inputs are uncertain.

What you get

Each stock gets its own folder with persistent, dated analysis files.
